Approach

How We Teach

Not exam prep. Not memorization. Pure understanding through interaction.

🎯
Interactive Visualizations
Drag points, adjust sliders, watch animations. Abstract concepts become tangible through hands-on exploration.
💡
Intuition First
Build deep understanding before formalism. Every theorem starts with "why" before "how to prove."
∑
Rigorous When Ready
KaTeX-rendered math with full proofs. Rigor scales with the tier — from playful to publication-grade.
🌱
Learn at Any Age
Five tiers from elementary school to research frontier. Start wherever you are, go as far as you want.
📈
Progress Tracking
Your learning progress is saved locally. Pick up right where you left off, across sessions.
🌐
Free & Open Source
Everything is on GitHub. No accounts, no paywalls, no ads. Just open the link and start learning.
