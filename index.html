Approach

How We Teach

Every concept gets the interactive treatment it deserves.

🎯
Interactive Visualizations
Drag points, adjust sliders, watch animations. Abstract concepts become tangible through hands-on exploration.
📝
Exercises & Solutions
Hundreds of exercises with progressive hints and full solutions. Test your understanding at every step.
∑
Rigorous Notation
KaTeX-rendered mathematics. No shortcuts on notation — every proof and definition is typeset properly.
⚡
Instant Loading
Lazy-loaded chapters with zero build step. Select a topic and start learning in milliseconds.
📈
Progress Tracking
Your learning progress is saved locally. Pick up right where you left off, across sessions.
🌐
Free & Open Source
Everything is on GitHub. No accounts, no paywalls. Just open the link and start learning.
